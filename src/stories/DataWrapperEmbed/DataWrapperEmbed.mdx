## Props

---

### `chartId` (required)  
**Type:** `string`  
The chart ID from Datawrapper (e.g. `csmgb` from the embed URL).

---

### `version`  
**Type:** `string`  
**Default:** `"1"`  
Version number of the chart, typically found in the embed URL (e.g. `2` in `/csmgb/2/`).

---

### `title`  
**Type:** `string`  
**Default:** `""`  
Accessible title for the iframe element.

---
